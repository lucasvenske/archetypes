Seven Sisters is a collection of illustrations centered around seven distinct feminine archetypes. Each artwork embodies the unique energy and symbolism of its archetype, utilizing vibrant colors and visual metaphors to capture their essence.

Dear sister, this project was created to help you understand the seven feminine archetypes through illustrations, inviting you to discover which of the sisters resides within you. You can incorporate these archetypes into your life with the help of our collection, fostering deep self-awareness and a connection to your own strengths and characteristics.
